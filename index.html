CourtClarity

Bridging the justice divide, one state at a time. Research updates, app progress, opportunities to join & give feedback.

About the project

CourtClarity uses human-centered design and language-model assistance to empower defendants with plain-language explanations, rights checklists, and easy access to resources. This site will host research updates, links to the app (App Store & Play), and ways to get involved.

Research timeline

Key dates and milestones

  • September 2025
    Shared the initial draft of Court Clarity with AFAM department peers, mentors, and constables and public defenders in Baton Rouge and New Orleans for feedback.
  • Oct 2025
    Public launch of Court Clarity for users in Baton Rouge and New Orleans, Louisiana.
  • Oct 2025
    Initiated collection of pre-arraignment survey data with users in Baton Rouge and New Orleans.
  • Nov 2025
    Begin post-arraignment data collection and conduct in-person interviews.
  • Dec 2025
    Complete post-arraignment data collection and finalize interview transcript preparation.
  • Spring 2026
    Conduct data analysis and draft thesis.
  • Spring 2026
    Present and defend thesis findings.
